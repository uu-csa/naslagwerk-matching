Do you have any further questions about the matching procedure? Further information can be found on our website. You can also check the website of the degree programme. On this page contact information can be found via Contact if you wish to call or email us.

PLEASE NOTE:

  1. If you do not take part in the matching activity, you will receive an e-mail stating our decision to reject your application. You will not be able to start your studies in our degree programme anymore.
  2. You must provide an active e-mail address in Studielink. In the period after your application, please check this address regularly for messages from the university. Also check whether any messages from the university may have gone to your spam box.
  3. If you completed the form early on, you often have the choice of more than one date in which to take part in the matching activity. If this is the case and if you should wish to do so, you will be able to adjust the date on the matching form prior to receiving the email-invitation to the activity. You can do this in OSIRIS Online Application.
