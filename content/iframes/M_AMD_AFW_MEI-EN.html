For this reason, we have to reject your application and withdraw it from Studielink.

Does an exemption category apply to you?

It is possible that the rejection is unjustified because one of the following exemptions applies to you:

  • You will receive a binding negative study advice (cf Article 7.8b of the WHW);
  • On 31 July, you will have obtained a minimum of 45 EC from another degree programme at Utrecht University and you wish to enrol in another degree programme.

If so, you may submit an exemption request. You can find the exemption request form in OSIRIS Online Application (see instructions below). Find your programme and click on the following link: "Click here to submit an exemption request (negative BSA or UU switch)". Please do so as soon as possible. The deadline for submitting an exemption request is 31 July at the latest. After this date, your exemption request will not be accepted.

Please note: you may already submit an exemption request even if you are currently not yet in possession of the official documents.

Lodging an objection

If none of the exemption categories apply to you, it is possible to lodge an objection to the Executive Board of Utrecht University within six weeks of receipt of this decision.

Please be aware that objections are only upheld in exceptional cases; factors such as holidays, travel or work will not be considered as circumstances beyond one's control. Other reasons, such as not being aware of the deadline, a late choice for a degree programme, exam stress, having entered a wrong date in your diary or not having been sure whether you would pass your exam, are not valid objections that will lead to a review of the decision.

You can lodge an objection by logging into OSIRIS Online Application (see instructions below). Find your programme and click the link: "Click here to submit an appeal against the rejection of your enrolment application due to application after deadline". In your objection, please elaborate on the reasons for not submitting the application form in time. We also ask you to upload any relevant previous e-mail correspondence and other supporting documents.
