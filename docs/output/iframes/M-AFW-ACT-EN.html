Possibility to appeal

If you do not agree with the decision as described in this email message, please lodge an objection to the Executive Board of Utrecht University within six weeks of receipt of this decision by submitting the objection form.

In your objection, please elaborate on the reasons for not participating in the matching activity. We would also ask you to upload all of the previous e-mail correspondence relating to the matching activity.

Please be aware that objections are only upheld in exceptional cases. Factors such as holidays, travel or work will not be considered as circumstances beyond one’s control. Other reasons, such as entering the wrong date in your diary, a late choice for a degree programme, exam stress or not having been sure whether you would pass your exams, are not valid objections to enable a review of the decision.
