What is matching?

Matching means that you will be comprehensively introduced to the degree programme of your choice before the start of your studies. You will have the opportunity to experience the programme before starting your studies, allowing you to obtain a realistic picture of what studying the programme entails. We will also discuss your motivation with you in order for you to find out whether the programme really suits you.

Participation in the matching procedure is a compulsory element of your application process. If you do not complete and submit the form on time, you will have failed to meet the matching obligation and your application will consequently be rejected. This means that you will not be able to start your studies.
