Matching

Participation in the matching procedure is a requirement for all future bachelor students. Because you have already participated in this procedure in the previous year, you are exempted from this requirement for the upcoming academic year (which starts 1 September 2019).

You do still need to complete your application request. This will be possible from June 2019 onwards. You will receive more information on this in June. If you have any questions concerning your Bachelor's programme, you should contact the Faculty Student Desk of your programme (via the website of our programme, select Contact).

Not the right choice?

In case you do not wish to start with this programme, please don't forget to cancel your application request in Studielink. If you are still in doubt whether you've made the right choice, please visit our our website.
